Explore our Exhibits

Virtual Reality

Step into a world of endless possibilities at our Virtual Reality exhibit! Get ready to explore exciting new realms, from underwater adventures to outer space missions.

Put on your VR headset and let your imagination soar as you interact with virtual environments and characters.

Robotics and AI

Welcome to the Robotics and AI exhibit, where the future comes to life!

Discover the incredible world of robots and artificial intelligence as you meet robots that can dance, draw, and even solve puzzles.

Cosmology

Explore the wonders of the cosmos. Our fantastic exhibition, ‘The Sky Above Us’, show you the night sky and what we can see and learn about the universe around us.

We’ll locate the various constellations and galaxies that can be seen.

Ecology

Step into the fascinating world of Ecology and embark on a journey to explore the wonders of our planet!

From lush rainforests to mysterious deep-sea ecosystems, you'll uncover the secrets of nature and learn how all living things are connected.
